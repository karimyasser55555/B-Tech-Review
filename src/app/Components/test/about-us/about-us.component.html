1997
We started under the name of "Olympic Stores" with approximately 65 employees and 3 branches only.
2000
Number of employees doubled into 158 and we reached 21 branches.
2003
The brand name got changed into "B.TECH" to reflect our vision and objective of providing high-quality technology of household & electronic appliances in every house with the purpose of facilitating people’s lives.
2011
The journey of success continued thanks to our employees in the first place. We reached 1295 employees and the number of branches grew into 56.
2018
Today, B.TECH is starting a new chapter of its success story after its 20th anniversary with 3000 employees in 70 branches, in 23 governorates and 28 Egyptian cities.
